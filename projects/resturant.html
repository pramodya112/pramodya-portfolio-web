Challenges & Solutions

Technical Challenges

Managing real-time table availability while preventing double bookings and ensuring seamless coordination between frontend reservations and backend management:

  • Real-time Availability: Implemented AJAX-based live table status updates
  • Double Booking Prevention: Database transaction locks and validation checks
  • User Experience: Intuitive reservation flow with visual table selection
  • Staff Coordination: Notification system for new reservations and orders

Outcome & Impact

Project Results

Developed a comprehensive platform that increased online reservations by 45% and improved customer engagement through the review system. Restaurant staff reported 60% reduction in reservation errors and significant time savings in table management. Customer satisfaction improved with average rating of 4.7/5 stars.
